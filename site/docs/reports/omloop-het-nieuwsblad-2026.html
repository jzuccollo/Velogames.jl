Fantasy retrospective — 28 February 2026

Cobbles & Wobbles won our fantasy league with 1027 points. But could they have done better? Read on to find out.

How the race played out

  • Riders who scored: 45 out of 175 starters
  • Top scorer: Mathieu Van Der Poel with 570 points
  • Best value: Tim Van Dijke at 46.5 pts/credit
  • Average value (all starters): 3.4 points per credit
  • Perfect team score: 2301 points for 92 credits
  • League winner: Cobbles & Wobbles with 1027 points
